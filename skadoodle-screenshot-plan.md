# Skadoodle — App Store Screenshot Plan (iPhone + iPad)

Keywords in play: doodle, drawing, diary, sketch, AI, gallery, art, journal, draw, finger, challenge, contest, prompt, daily, vote

Same story/order for both device sizes — capture each shot fresh on iPhone (6.9") and iPad (13") rather than stretching one set. First 2–3 shots carry the most weight for conversion, so lead with the hook, not the feature list.

---

## 1. Hero — the drawing itself
**Headline:** "Doodle Anything. Finger, Pencil, or Pen."
**Screen:** Mid-doodle on the canvas, a colorful in-progress drawing, pen picker visible showing a few distinctive styles (Watercolor, Neon, or one of the Dual Tone styles like Braid/Tube — something that doesn't look like every other drawing app).
**Why:** First impression = "this is a real drawing tool," not a toy. Hits draw / drawing / sketch / finger directly.

## 2. AI magic moment
**Headline:** "AI Gets What You Drew"
**Screen:** The result card right after tapping Done — a doodle with its AI-generated caption and tags visible.
**Why:** AI is a strong differentiator and a named keyword; this is the single most "wait, it does that?" moment in the app.

## 3. Daily Doodle hero card
**Headline:** "One Prompt a Day. Everyone Doodles It."
**Screen:** The Today tab's gold-framed "Doodle of the Day" winner card, ribbon and all, with the day's subject visible on the placard.
**Why:** Hits daily / prompt / challenge / contest all at once — this is your most distinctive feature and should get a premium showcase, not just a screenshot of the tab.

## 4. Voting booth
**Headline:** "Vote On Yesterday's Doodles"
**Screen:** The full-screen voting booth mid-swipe, Yes/No buttons visible, subject capsule at top.
**Why:** Hits vote / contest directly, and shows the app has a real interactive loop, not just solo drawing.

## 5. Community gallery
**Headline:** "A Gallery Built By Doodlers"
**Screen:** World Gallery grid, a lively mix of styles/subjects, maybe with the likes/comments visible on one tile.
**Why:** Hits gallery / art directly, establishes there's an actual audience here, not a ghost town.

## 6. Personal history / timelapse
**Headline:** "Watch Every Doodle Come to Life"
**Screen:** Private gallery grid with the timelapse play badge visible on a tile, or a frame from an actual timelapse video mid-playback.
**Why:** Hits diary / journal / sketch from the "this is your personal creative record" angle — timelapse is a genuinely unique feature worth its own beat.

## 7. Pen variety
**Headline:** "Every Style You Can Imagine"
**Screen:** Pen Studio sheet open, showing the full grid of pen styles (Watercolor, Confetti, Calligraphy, Trim, Tube, etc.) — the breadth is the point.
**Why:** Reinforces drawing / sketch / art as genuinely deep, not a gimmick with 3 pens.

## 8. Closer — the pitch
**Headline:** "One Price. No Ads. Ever."
**Screen:** Either a clean app-icon/brand card, or the profile screen looking polished (banner + doodles).
**Why:** Direct trust/differentiation close — most drawing apps are ad-heavy or subscription-gated; naming that plainly is a real selling point worth its own slide.

## App Store Description (draft 2 — rewritten from scratch)

No artistic skill required. That's the whole point.

Skadoodle gives you exotic pens you won't find anywhere else — watercolor, chalk, neon, spray, confetti, braided rope, calligraphy, and many more — plus real layering, so you can stack, hide, and reorder your work like a professional canvas. Mix in emoji, text, and photos right alongside your drawing: extract objects straight out of your own pictures and stamp them into the scene.

When you're done, AI looks at your doodle and writes a caption for you — keep it or make it your own.

Save everything to your personal gallery, or post your favorites to the live world gallery and see what people everywhere are drawing right now. Like, comment, and follow the artists you love.

## App Store Description (draft 1 — kept for reference)

Skadoodle is a doodle sketchbook with a live community gallery. Draw anything, and AI instantly writes a caption and tags your doodle. Keep it private, or post it for the world to see.

No artistic skill required. That's the whole point.

**Draw**
Use your finger or Apple Pencil to sketch on a clean canvas. Choose from lots of exotic pens — pencil, ink, brush, marker, chalk, neon, spray, watercolor, braid, confetti, and more. Adjust color and width, undo freely, set a photo or solid color as your background.

**Stamps**
Drop emoji, text, or photo stamps onto your canvas. Extract subjects from your own photos and stamp them right into your doodle. Text stamps support 12 fonts with bold, italic, and alignment options.

**AI Captions**
When you're done, Skadoodle's AI looks at your doodle and writes a caption. Edit it or keep it as-is.

**Your Gallery**
Every doodle you save goes into your personal gallery. Swipe through your history anytime.

**World Gallery**
Post your favorites to the live world gallery. See what people everywhere are drawing right now. Like and comment on the ones you love. Follow your favorite artists and see their latest work in your feed.
